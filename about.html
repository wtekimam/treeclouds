Our Story

"Tree Clouds is committed to develop a digital solutions for companies and private businesses alike.
Tree Clouds is an Digital Agency focused on creating and/or improving digital presence for businesses. Our services include creating mobile-friendly websites, apps, and also digital marketing.
Our approach focuses on new ways to integrate technology into existing or new businesses. We work closely with clients in which each client will have one dedicated account manager that can interpret their businesses' needs, identifying opportunities and minimizing risks.
We believe that a digital presence is a process of continuous growth and our team helps clients not only on one off projects, but also long-term identity building. Long-term contracts help clients to grow their digital identity not only for a while but for it to last for years and years to come."

Our Process

These are some of our process

Requirement Gathering

We meet to understand what your website is all about, what are the goals you are trying to achieve

Planning

Making plans from the requirements gathered and make a sitemap and flow, also making sure that you are using the correct solution

Development

Design is turned into a fully functioning website. Creating database, layout and filling in contents

Design

Creating wireframes from the flows and sitemap and our designers create a mock up design according to the specifications

Testing

Test to check the full functionality of the web, making sure that the website is beautiful from all browser and devices

Launch

The website is live and can be accessed publicly
